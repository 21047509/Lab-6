Doraemon

Doraemon is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Fujiko F. Fujio. The series follows the adventures of a robotic cat named Doraemon, who travels back in time from the 22nd century to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi navigate through the challenges of everyday life. Doraemon is equipped with a variety of futuristic gadgets, such as a pocket that can produce any item he needs, a time machine, and a device that allows him to teleport to any location. The series has been widely popular in Japan and has been translated into numerous languages, making it one of the most recognized manga and anime franchises in the world.
